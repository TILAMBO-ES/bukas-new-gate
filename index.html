About Project B.U.K.A.S.

Building Upgrades for Knowledge, Access, & Safety (B.U.K.A.S.) represents a collaborative milestone initiative executed during the 2025-2026 school year. Funded entirely through community efforts, the upgrades ensure a safer, highly accessible, and structurally sound learning ecosystem for the students and faculty of Tilambo Elementary School.
